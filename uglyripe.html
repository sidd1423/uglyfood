Ugly is the New Beautiful

We started UglyRipe because we couldn't stand watching perfectly good food go to waste. Here's our story.

Our Mission

Fighting Food Waste, One Wonky Carrot at a Time

Every year, nearly one-third of all food produced globally is thrown away — and a staggering portion of that waste happens before food ever reaches a store shelf, simply because it doesn't look perfect enough. A lemon too lumpy. An apple with a small bump. A carrot that grew in two directions at once.

At UglyRipe, our mission is to challenge the absurd beauty standards placed on produce by connecting conscious consumers directly with farms that have perfectly nutritious, perfectly delicious food that would otherwise end up in landfills. We believe that taste, nutrition, and sustainability should always come before aesthetics. Every box you buy is a vote for a more sensible, compassionate food system.
